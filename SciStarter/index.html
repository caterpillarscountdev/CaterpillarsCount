What is SciStarter?

And what does it have to do with Caterpillars Count?

SciStarter connects particiants to new and exciting citizen science projects and lets them track their participation across multiple projects right in one place! How do you use it? We're glad you asked!

  • Visit SciStarter's website and sign up for an account with them, using the same email address as your Caterpillars Count! account.
  • Find all sorts of projects that interest you and request to join them. There is no need to request Caterpillars Count! if you already have an account with us.
  • Conveniently track your future partication for all your projects right on your SciStarter Dashboard!
