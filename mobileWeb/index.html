Caterpillars Count! Mobile Website

Our website works on your phone or tablet to conduct surveys or manage your account. For ease and offline field use we recommend adding it to your home screen.

Adding to your Home Screen

Safari on Apple iOS

  • Launch Safari.
  • Open our website, if you're not already here.
  • Tap the Action or Share icon at the bottom of the screen.
  • Select "Add to Home Screen" - you may need to swipe up from the bottom of the screen with two fingers to reveal additional options.
  • Tap on "Add" at the top right corner of the screen to finish and add the icon to your home screen.

Chrome on Google Android

  • Launch Chrome.
  • Open our website, if you're not already here.
  • Tap the 3 dots menu by the address bar.
  • Select "Add to Home Screen".
  • Tap "Install" to finish and add the icon to your home screen.
